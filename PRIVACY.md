# Privacy Policy — Look Inventory

_Last updated: 2026_

Look Inventory is an offline-first app for shopkeepers. **Your data is yours.**

## What we collect
**Nothing.** There is no server operated by us and no analytics. Everything you enter — items, prices, sales, customers, Khata balances — is stored **only on your device** (in the browser's IndexedDB).

## Optional cloud backup / sync
If you choose to turn on **Google Drive backup** or **Firebase live sync**, a copy of your data is sent to **your own** Google or Firebase account so you can restore it or sync between your phones. We do not have access to those accounts or your data. You can stop and delete this at any time from those accounts.

## Customer information
If you save a customer's name or phone number (for Khata or B2B billing), it is used only on your device and only to send WhatsApp reminders that **you** choose to send. Get your customers' consent before saving their details, as required by local law.

## Permissions
- **Camera** — used only for barcode scanning, when you start it.
- **Microphone** — used only for voice billing, when you tap the mic.
- **Storage** — used to keep your data on the device.

None of these are used in the background or for tracking.

## Your responsibility
You are responsible for the accuracy of your records and for meeting any tax or legal obligations (e.g. GST). The app is provided "as is", without warranty.
